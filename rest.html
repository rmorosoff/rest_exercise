Rudy's Top 6 Seattle Restaurants

  1. Wild Ginger - Wild Ginger is by far the best Thai place in town, and one of the best ANYWHERE
  2. The Pink Door - You need to see this place to believe it. Great food. Even COOLER atmosphere
  3. The Metropolitan Grill - Best steak in town. Enough said
  4. Ferry Noodle House - The Ferry Noodle House is a cool little (and affordable) spot on the way to the Ferry Terminal
  5. Cafe Nola - Take the ferry over to Bainbridge Island and enjoy the view as well as some great food
  6. Rudy's Place - Rudy can't cook worth a damn, but his wife can. And Rudy mixes some POTENT cocktails
